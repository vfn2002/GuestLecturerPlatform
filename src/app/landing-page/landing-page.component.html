How the process works

chevron_right Sign up on the platform as a professional, lecturer or student. Lecturers and students can sign up with their school emails. chevron_right As a professional fill in your expertise, topics you would like to share with the students and availability. As a lecturer, share the description of the course. We will make sure to match you to the right professional. As a student you can see upcoming events, sign up for guest lectures or request new ones. chevron_right As a lecturer, browse through the pool of professionals, see their availability and book them right away. The professional will receive a notification and can accept or reject it. If you want to exchange more information with the professional, use the chat option.
